Culebra E-Bike Map - Project Notes
====================================

Project: Interactive e-bike guest map for Culebra, Puerto Rico
Owner: Isla Tech PR
Repository: TodoCulebra/Culebra-map

Purpose:
- Provide e-bike rental guests with a digital map of Culebra island
- Highlight key destinations, safe routes, and points of interest
- Improve the guest experience with easy-to-follow navigation

Key Locations to Include:
- Flamenco Beach
- Tamarindo Beach
- Zoni Beach
- Melones Beach
- Culebra National Wildlife Refuge
- Town of Dewey (main town)
- Ferry terminal
- Airport
- Restaurants and local shops

Development Notes:
- Map should be mobile-friendly (guests will use phones while riding)
- Keep load times fast for areas with limited cell service
- Consider offline capability for remote parts of the island

Status: Initial setup
Date Started: 2026-03-06
